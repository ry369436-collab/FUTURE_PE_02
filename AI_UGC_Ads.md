🧠 Phase 1: Prompt Engineering Framework

The following prompt layout dictates constraints, baseline parameters, and corporate positioning to force high-
conversion peer-to-peer outputs from the LLM:
Act as an expert DTC and Local Business Performance Marketer specializing in UGC (User Generated Content) ad scripts for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. 

I need a complete UGC Ad Content Pack for a local Dental Clinic. The clinic's unique selling propositions (USPs) are: 
1) A strict "No-Guilt, No-Judgment" policy for patients who haven't been to the dentist in years.
2) Affordable, transparent pricing.
3) Advanced pain-free technology.

Deliver the following structured outputs:
1. 3 Psychology-driven, "scroll-stopping" hooks tested for 3-second retention.
2. 2 Complete Short-Form Video Scripts (60 seconds) following the Problem-Solution-CTA framework, including visual/audio cues.
3. Platform-specific variants for Instagram/Facebook and YouTube Shorts.
4. Optimized captions with high-intent CTAs and hashtag strategies.

Tone: Authentic, casual, empathetic, peer-to-peer (NOT corporate or clinical).
